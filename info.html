Who made this

Built by ValTheOneAndOnly — a solo developer who got tired of Windows bloat and wanted one clean tool to toggle everything. No startups, no subscriptions, no bullshit. Just a portable exe and a purple thunderstorm.

What it does

57 registry + service tweaks across CPU, GPU, System, Network, and Privacy. Toggle any tweak and it applies immediately. Revert any tweak just as fast. Auto-creates a System Restore Point before your first change. Live CPU/GPU/RAM/Disk monitoring with sparkline graphs. State saved per-user in the registry — close and reopen, your toggles are still there.
